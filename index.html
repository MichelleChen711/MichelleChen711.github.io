No More Hangry

No More Hangry is a food web app that simplifies the struggle of deciding what to eat.

Nowadays, people are plagued with the question: "What do I want to eat?" Sites like Seamless and GrubHub give us too many choices and too many steps. No More Hangry has the solution. Introducing the new food app that makes decisions easier!

If we are asked whether we want Mexican food, chances are, we aren't so sure. But, if we are asked whether we want a steak burrito for 5 dollars, then decision is much easier.

No More Hangry eliminates the top down search structure of selecting a food category, then a restaurant from that food category, to an entree in that restaurant. By showing the food first, No More Hangry focuses on the important deciding factors: what entree looks good and its price.

Create an account, save your address, payment details, and preferences once and No More Hangry will do the rest. For example, an image of a delicious entree under $10 with at least 3 stars will be displayed to you. Dont like it? Click "Randomize" to see another entree. If you are satisfied with the current entree, all you have to do is click the "Order Now" button and the food is on its way to your doorstep.

  • Platform: Web
  • Date: Fall 2015
  • Created With: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, Express, AJAX, MongoDB, SASS
  • Visit Here: No More Hangry

EatUp

EatUp is an iOS application created on the principle that planning meals should be about choosing friends, not dates and times. It is the optimal tool to consistently bring busy friends together by calculating the best time to meet for everyone with just a push of a button. In addition, it has a built-in restaurant voting system that syncs with yelp.

Ultimately, our goal is to provide friends who want to keep in touch with a non-intrusive, easy-to-use tool to check meal opportunities together. As students ourselves, we understand that the complexity of our schedules can sometimes discourage us from even trying. Now, with EatUp, we can experience the new simplicity in planning meet ups with friends.

EatUp is a NYU student-created startup and was a semi-finalist in the 2016 NYU $200k Entrepreneur's Challenge Competition. (Previously known as MealPlan)

  • Platform: iOS
  • Date: Summer 2015 - Present
  • Created With: Swift
  • Visit Here: www.eatup-app.com MealPlan
